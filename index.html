Alphabetz Coaching Center: Nurturing Young Minds

The institution is established on December 20, 2022 and has rapidly grown into a preferred choice for students from classes 1 to 12. Catering to the diverse educational needs of students, the center offers comprehensive coaching for West Bengal Board, CBSE, and ICSE curricula.

Education for humans by humans

With a team of 15 highly qualified and experienced teachers, Alphabetz prioritizes individual attention for each student. By assigning dedicated teachers for every subject, the center ensures specialized guidance. Its focus on effective teaching methodologies, coupled with regular assessments through monthly tests, helps students grasp concepts clearly.

We excel together

Recognizing the challenges faced by weaker students, the center offers extra classes to bridge knowledge gaps. Furthermore, the coaching center's flexibility in teaching languages – English, Bengali, and Hindi – accommodates diverse student needs. In a short span, Alphabetz has garnered a reputation for its student-centric approach and commitment to academic excellence.
